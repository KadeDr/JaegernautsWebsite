What do we do?

Based out of Memorial Early College High School (MECHS) in New Braunfels, Texas, we have the mission of empowering high schoolers in their learning endeavors. As a robotics team, our priorities are not strictly set on winning the competition or getting the shiniest medal, but rather learning and innovating on the way to the top. We value leadership, craftsmanship, reliability, loyalty, and education and aspire to promote all of these values to everybody in this small high school. We compete in two events that take place in Texas: FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) and BEST Robotics Competition (BEST).
